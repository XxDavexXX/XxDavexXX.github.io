YOU CAN'T MISS THIS ADVENTURE

Canoeing, rafting or canoeing is a water sport that consists of entering the river, sea inside a raft called Kayak or rubber boat and facing the speed of the water currents. Because Peru has a special geography and more than a dozen rivers that exceed 600 km in length, rafting is a very popular sport or Adventure Tourism in our country.


What to take on your first adventure trip?

RAFTING

It is probably one of the activities that most terrifies most. But it is safer than you can imagine, if you are accompanied by a specialist. For that, you must bring:

- some beach shorts
- a waterproof pole
- dry clothes for after canoeing
- water shoes or slippers that you can get wet
- Make sure that the boating agency offers you:
- a raft in good condition
- a protective helmet
- a life jacket in your size

Where can I do it?

Canoeing is not only practiced in the Cañete River (Lunahuaná), the Camaná River, the Chili River or the Apurímac River, there is a great variety in our country, just click to learn more rivers.
